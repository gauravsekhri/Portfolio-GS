AI Vs Web Development : My Experience
Digital marketing has increasingly made it difficult for students to pursue their interests or to keep up with what's in demand. In my early days of college, I enjoyed playing with CSS for fun. Creating clones of websites has always been one of my favorite activities. But now there is something called artificial intelligence that has suddenly gained popularity.

I was one of those students who were heavily influenced by the marketing efforts of renowned institutions to take on AI courses. No doubt, it was a way to capture the attention of the students. After beginning to learn machine learning and its advanced version, deep learning, I realized there are infinite topics within it. There are tons of sub-topics within every topic.

Making decisions on your own when you do not have any mentor makes all career choices extremely hard. I was unsure if I should seek out artificial intelligence because it is trending in markets and may help me in my future, or if I should pursue my interest.

Finally, I decided to work hard so that I could see myself as a full-stack developer.
